Classic Homemade Lasagna Recipe

This hearty and comforting lasagna recipe features layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese for the ultimate Italian-inspired dish.

The meat sauce is made with ground beef and Italian sausage simmered with tomatoes, garlic, and herbs, while the béchamel adds a velvety texture.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 lb (450g) Italian sausage (mild or spicy)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • ½ cup red wine (optional, but adds depth)
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar (balances acidity)
  • 2 bay leaves

Cheese Layers

  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg (helps bind the ricotta)
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (divided)
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tsp dried)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper

Steps

Meat Sauce Preparation

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spoon (about 5-7 minutes). Drain excess fat.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until translucent (about 3 minutes), then add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and red wine (if using). Mix well.
  4. Add basil, oregano, salt, pepper, sugar, and bay leaves. Stir to combine.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sauce thickens. Remove bay leaves before assembling.

Cheese Mixture Preparation

  1. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, 1 cup of mozzarella, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix until well blended.
